## Usage notes

- **Merge fields:** `[First name]`, `[Company]`, and the `[ramping a new line / hiring controls engineers]` trigger — set from the lead's public signal (job posting, press release, SI Giants listing).
- **Cadence:** first touch → 5-day follow-up → ~5 days later, the scenario email. No reply after three → move to quarterly nurture.
- **Single-CTA rule:** one action per email; never stack call + profiles + signup in one.
- **Compliance:** B2B cold email — include a physical address and one-click unsubscribe to stay CAN-SPAM compliant.
